$$
\begin{cases}
\lambda > 0: &\Rightarrow & f(x) = \sin(\sqrt{\lambda}y) + \cos(\sqrt{\lambda }y) \\
\lambda < 0: &\Rightarrow & f(x) = e^{\pm\sqrt{\lambda}y}
\end{cases}
