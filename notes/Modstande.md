$$V_{ab}=V_a - V_b$$

Følger [[Ohm's Lov]]

If the current through a resistor increases, the voltage increases.

### Strøm
Strøm gennem en modstand
$$I_{a \rightarrow b} = \frac{V_a - V_b}{R} = \frac{V_{ab}}{R}$$
