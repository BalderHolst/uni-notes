## Discrete
Joint mass function
$$
f(x, y) = \mathbf{P}(X = x\ \mathrm{and}\ Y = y)
$$

---

## Continuous
The function $f(x, y)$ is a PDF for the random variables $X$ and $Y$ if:
$$
f(x, y) \geq 0,\quad \forall(x, y)
$$
and
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}
f(x,y)\, dx\,dy = 1
$$
and
$$
\mathbf{P}(X,Y) \in A = \iint_{A}f(x,y)\,dx\,dy,
\quad \mathrm{for\; any}\; A \subset \mathbb{R}^2
$$
