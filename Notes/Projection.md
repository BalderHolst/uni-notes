# Projection
Project points in a space $S$ into a subspace $U$.

$$
P = A(A^{T}A)^{-1}A^{T}
$$
$P$: Projection matrix
$A$: Mapping into subspace

Now points in $S$ space can be projected to $U$ with
$$
x_{U} = Px
$$
$x$: Original point
$x_U$: Projected point
