# Normal Distribution
Propability Density Function (pdf):
$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi}} \exp \left\{ -\frac{1}{2 \sigma^{2}}(x - \mu)^{2} \right\}, \quad x \in \mathbb{R}
$$
$\mu$: Mean
$\sigma^2$: [[Variance]]

$$
z = \frac{\bar{x}-\mu_{0}}{\sigma / \sqrt{n}} \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 1)
$$
