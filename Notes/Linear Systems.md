### Linear Time Invariant System
$$
\begin{align}
f(x+y) &= f(x) + f(y) &\Rightarrow \text{Linear} \\
f(\alpha x) &= \alpha f(x) &\Rightarrow \text{Time Invariant}
\end{align}
$$
