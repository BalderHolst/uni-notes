### Rodrigues' formula
Calculate the equivalent rotation matrix.

$$
^A_BR(\ ^A\hat{K}, \theta\ ) = I \cos(\theta) + KK^{-1} (1 - \cos(\theta)) + \hat{K} \sin(\theta)
$$
