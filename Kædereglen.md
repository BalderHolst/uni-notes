$$f(x) = (3x^2+x)^7$$
Define $u$
$$u(x)^7, \s u(x) = 3x^2+x$$
This is the formula 
$$\frac{d}{dx}f(x) = \frac{df}{du} \cdot \frac{du}{dx}$$
$$\Updownarrow$$
$$\frac{d}{dx} f(x)= \frac{d}{du}(u^7) \cdot \frac{d}{dx}(3x^2+x) = 7u^6 \cdot 6x+1$$
Substituting for $u$
$$f'(x)=7 \cdot (3x^2+x) \cdot 6x +1$$
