# Dynamic Compensation
See [[Lecture 7 - Dynamic Compensators and Stability Margins.pdf#page=24|slides]].

### Lead Compensator
$$
D(s) = \frac{Ts + 1}{\alpha Ts + 1} \quad \mathrm{where}\ \alpha < 1
$$

### Lag Compensator
$$
D(s) = K_{0}\alpha \frac{Ts + 1}{\alpha Ts + 1}
$$
