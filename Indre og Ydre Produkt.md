$$\vec{a} = \left(
\begin{array}{c}
 3 \\
 6 \\
 1 \\
\end{array}
\right) \s \vec{b}=\left(
\begin{array}{c}

### Indre Produkt
$$\vec{a}^{T} \cdot \vec{b} = \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 6 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right) \cdot  \left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1 \\
 2 \\
 -4 \\
\end{array}
\right) = 11$$

### Ydre produkt
$$\vec{a}^{T} \cdot \vec{b} = \left(
\begin{array}{c}
 3 \\
 6 \\
 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)
\cdot
\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 1 & 2 & -4 \\
\end{array}
\right) = \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 6 & -12 \\
 6 & 12 & -24 \\
 1 & 2 & -4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$$
